Let's help Puerto Rico
On September 22nd 2017 a category-4 hurricane named "Hurricane Irma" struck many countries in the American continent including causing a devastating situation in an island called "Puerto Rico"
As the worst storm to hit the island in almost 90 years—it left it completely without power. It could take months to restore access to electricity, while a public health crisis is in danger of emerging as huge swathes of the population don’t have access to safe drinking water. As that was not enough as the countries economy is in a continuous downfall since '04.

Bronx Academy For Software Engineering is extending their hand by doing a fund raising on helping the ones in need, Students are participating on making fliers, posters and many motivational pieces. Many of our friends and families are from Puerto Rico, and it's time for us to stand by them.
